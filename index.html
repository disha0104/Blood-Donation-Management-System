What is this all about ?


We solve the problem of blood emergencies by linking
blood donors directly with people in need of blood.

What we do ?

We connect blood donors with recipients, without any intermediary such as blood banks, for an efficient and seamless process.


Innovative

Life Savers is an innovative approach to address global health.We provide immediate access to blood donors.

Network

Life Savers is one of several community organizations working together as a network that responds to emergencies in an efficient manner.

Get notified

Life Savers Connect works with network partners to connect blood donors and recipients through an automated SMS service and a mobile app.

Totally Free

Life Savers ultimate goal is to provide an easy -to-use, easy-to-access, fast, efficient, and reliable way to get life-saving blood, totally Free of cost.

Save Life

We are a non profit foundation and our main objective is to make sure that everything is done to protect vulnerable persons.Help us by making a gift!
